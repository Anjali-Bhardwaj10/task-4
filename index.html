HISTORY OF BIHARI CULTURE

Bihar is renowned for its rich tradition of performing arts, with folk songs, folk dances, and classical music being an integral part of its culture.
The state is also famous for its handicrafts, such as Madhubani and Mithila paintings, Tussar silk sarees, and bamboo and leather works.
